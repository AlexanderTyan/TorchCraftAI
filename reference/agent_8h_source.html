19 /**
20  * An Agent represents the micromanagement state of one of our units.
21  *
22  * Agents belong to a SquadTask, which invokes microFlee() or microDelete().
23  *
24  * From there, the Agent, will forward control to a series of Behaviors,
25  * each of which is given the opportunity to do one of the following:
26  * * Post a sharp UPC (for consumption as an in-game command by UPCToCommand)
27  * * Do nothing (issue no commands to the unit this frame)
28  * * Defer control: give the next Behavior an opportunity to control the unit
29  */
